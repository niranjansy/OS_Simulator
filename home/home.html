OS Simulator

By students of NITK CSE Batch 2022


Goal

To demonstrate the working of a typical Operating System by simulating the underlying algorithms


Objectives

  • To explain algorithms related to the following concepts in Operating Systems:
    1. Disk Scheduling
    2. Process Scheduling
    3. File Allocation
    4. Memory Allocation
    5. Deadlocks

  • To demonstrate the working of the above algorithms through simulation
